About me

Let me introduce myself to you

Hi there, my name is Kouah Mohammed Aymen. I am an algerian computer science student at "National Computer Science Engineering School, Oued Smar, Algiers, Algeria". I come from the city of Ain Beida in the east of the country. I am a front-end developer, a motion graphics designer and somewhat of a photographer (I use phones mainly). I also enjoy writing scripts and directing as well as programming. I believe I am an enthusiastic and hard working individual who's always open to trying and learning new stuff. My lack of experience can be made up for by my ability to adapt quickly and grasp new concepts in short periods of time as well as my willingness to always push forward and widen the range of my skills. I speak 3 languages: Arabic, English and French (in order of descending fluency) as well as some Spanish and Japanese expressions; So communication isn't a problem for me most of the time. If you are willing to overlook my inexperince and focus more on my strengths and abilities, please do contact me through my email: jm_kouah@esi.dz or through the form in contact page.
